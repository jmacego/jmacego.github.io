# Era-bridge — vintage and modern hardware

**Use when:** the AI vs. DotCom essay series is the active campaign, or any
time the banner is making a "things repeat" argument. The banner1 image
already in `image_drop/` (CRT + flatscreen with topographic glow) is an
example of this category.

**Composition rules:**
- 4:1 aspect ratio
- Old hardware on the left, modern hardware on the right (or vice versa) —
  but leave one side relatively empty for text
- Brand palette: lime + violet glows reading off the screens
- Photographic, dim, late-night-workspace energy
- No people, no readable on-screen content, no logos

## Prompt to paste into OpenAI Images 2.0

> Wide cinematic 4:1 photograph (1584 × 396), a 1990s beige CRT monitor and a modern thin OLED display sharing the same dark wooden desk, both glowing faintly green and violet against a near-black room, no readable text on either screen, no people, atmospheric with deep shadow, lime green (#9AF23C) and violet (#A78BFA) ambient light spill, 35mm film realism, generous negative space across the right two-thirds of the frame.

## Variants to try

If you want the vintage vibe stronger:

> ...add a beige mechanical keyboard, a faint scan-line texture on the CRT, a coffee mug ring on the wood, low-key tungsten desk lamp providing the only warm light...

If you want the modern side more dominant:

> ...modern OLED display three times larger than the CRT, foreground depth-of-field on the modern screen, the CRT slightly out of focus in the background-left...

If you want pure abstraction (just the screens, no desk):

> ...two screens floating in pure black void, isolated on plinths, museum-style display lighting from above, cinematic 4:1...
