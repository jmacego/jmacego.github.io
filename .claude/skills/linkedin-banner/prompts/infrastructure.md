# Infrastructure / fiber / data-center

**Use when:** evergreen positioning banner, anything tech-leadership-flavoured,
or when the user is unsure. This is the safe default.

**Composition rules baked into the prompt:**
- 4:1 aspect ratio (LinkedIn banner)
- Generous negative space on the right two-thirds (text lands there)
- Brand palette: deep neutrals with lime + violet accent glows
- No people, no readable text, no logos
- Photographic realism, not stock-photo glossy

## Prompt to paste into OpenAI Images 2.0

> Ultra-wide cinematic photograph, 4:1 aspect ratio (1584 × 396), looking down a long dark server room corridor at dawn, fiber optic strands glowing faintly with pale lime green (#9AF23C) and violet (#A78BFA) light against deep matte charcoal surfaces (#0E0F10 to #15171A), subtle volumetric haze, narrow depth of field, no people, no readable text, no logos, generous empty space across the right two-thirds of the frame, moody and contemplative not commercial, 35mm film grain, dark photographic realism.

## Variants to try

If the first generation puts the focal point too central, retry with:

> ...generous empty space across the **right three-quarters** of the frame, focal interest concentrated in the **far left quarter only**...

If the lime feels too saturated:

> ...subtle pale lime green and violet **highlights only on metal surfaces**, predominantly cool charcoal tones...

If you want a colder, more "data center" feel:

> ...replace dawn lighting with cold cyan emergency lighting, faint lime indicators on rack switches, predominantly black and dark slate.
